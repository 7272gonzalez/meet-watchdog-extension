2. Description of Service

Meet Watchdog is a Chrome browser extension that monitors your Google Calendar and alerts you when a video call meeting (Google Meet, Zoom, or Microsoft Teams) is active but you have not joined. The Extension operates entirely within your browser and does not transmit your data to any third-party server operated by the developer.

3. Google Account and Calendar Access
